# Void Dungeon NPC Roster — Recolor Feasibility & Build Plan

Your instincts were right on both counts. **Void spiders are real Tier A** (the spider sprite is 100% gray-ramped — proven by the 6 shipped color variants), and **void giants are the textbook Tier A case** (Moss/Ice/Fire giants are the *same body* with different color fields — the in-game proof). You can stand up a coherent void dungeon mostly from 5-minute color swaps, then bake a handful of creature sprites for flavor.

One important correction from the empirical pixel checks: **spiders, rats, wolves, and unicorns are Tier A, not B** — their creature sprites are gray-ramped and tint via `charColour`. But **skeletons, zombies, ghosts, hobgoblins, and the Nazastarool Zombie are baked palettes (Tier B)** — they need per-frame baking. Verdicts are folded into the tables below.

## Recommended BUILD ORDER (biggest, cheapest win first)

Do these **6 color-only swaps first** — every one is ~5 minutes, no baking, and together they populate trash → mid → mini-boss → boss so the dungeon is *playable end-to-end* before you touch a single pixel:

| # | NPC → Void name | id | Role filled | Cost |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Moss Giant → **Void Giant** | 104 | mid (the proof-of-concept; validates your tint pipeline) | color fields |
| 2 | Giant Spider → **Void Weaver** | 74 | trash (your requested void spider; 100% gray) | charColour |
| 3 | Hellhound → **Voidhound** | 296 | mini-boss (highest combat beast; purple wolf already ships) | charColour |
| 4 | Darkwizard → **Void Cultist** | 60 | caster (already dark-robed) | robe fill + 2 indices |

| 6 | Black Dragon → **Void Wyrm** | 292 | boss (dragon family is one tintable sprite) | new `dragon` AnimationDef |

**Why this set:** it spans every role with the four *proven-tintable families* (giant body, spider, wolf, dragon) plus the cheapest caster, so you validate all your recolor levers at once. After this plays well, add the **equipment-fill minibosses** (Black Knight 66 / Paladin 323, reusing the Void Knight 853 fill pattern), then invest baking time in the **undead set** (skeleton 40 + skeleton mage 499 + zombie 41 → `void_bake.py`) for the thematic crypt payoff.

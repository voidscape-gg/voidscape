Community rules

By creating an account, you agree to these rules for public chat, private messages, names, and every other player-created submission.

Play fairVoidscape is a shared world. Treat other players like people.
1

Keep It Safe

No hate speech, harassment, threats, sexual or exploitative content, illegal content, doxxing, or sharing another person's private information.

2

Keep It Honest

No impersonation, scams, malicious links, spam, cheating services, account theft, or attempts to evade moderation and game-integrity controls.

3

Report And Block

Use Report a player for rule-breaking content and the Ignore list to block a player. For urgent safety or account issues, email support@voidscape.gg.

4

Enforcement

We may remove content and warn, mute, suspend, or close accounts. Serious or repeated violations may be acted on without advance warning. Reports are reviewed in context.

Privacy

Data deletion

Request account data deletion.

Use this process for Voidscape portal accounts, prelaunch reservations, Google-linked web accounts, and account-management records.

Manual review We verify account ownership before deleting data so another player cannot erase your account.
Step 1

Send The Request

Email support@voidscape.gg from the email address on the account. Include your portal email, character names you want reviewed, and whether you want portal-only deletion or full account closure where possible.

Step 2

Verify Ownership

Staff may ask for a recovery-code proof, a recent account action, or another private confirmation. Do not send your password. We will never ask for it.

Step 3

Deletion And Retention

After verification, we delete or anonymize portal account data that is no longer needed. We may retain limited security, abuse-prevention, payment, entitlement, server-log, or game-integrity records when needed to prevent fraud, investigate abuse, preserve fair play, or satisfy legal obligations.

Step 4

Confirmation

When the request is complete, staff will confirm by email or the official support channel used for the request. If a connected provider such as Google is involved, you can also remove Voidscape access from that provider's own account settings.

Prelaunch feature guide

RuneScape Classic, expanded where it counts.

Voidscape keeps the old combat timing, the social friction, the supply pressure, the risk, and the full world scale, then adds faster launch progression, a sharper client, new PvM and PvP loops, real account tools, and transparency you can verify for yourself.

XP and progression

Combat moves fast. Skilling still earns its weight.

The launch rates stay deliberately simple: combat moves quickly enough to explore fights and builds, while skilling stays slow enough that supplies, gathering routes, and player markets still matter.

Combat XP 10x

Melee, ranged, magic, hits, and prayer all progress at a pace built for active launch play.

Skilling XP 1.5x

Gathering and production run faster than stock Classic without flattening the economy.

Subscribed 11x / 2.5x

An active subscription adds +1x to your combat and skilling rates.

Rested XP 1.5x

Time offline banks up to 45 minutes of bonus training.

Game tick 640ms

Classic movement and combat cadence stay authentic.

Fatigue Off

No fatigue wall cutting your skilling sessions short.

Starter paths

Pick Warrior, Forager, or Arcanist on Void Island. Each path gives a focused early 2x boost to its starter skills until they hit level 50.

Milestone rewards

Skill and total-level milestones pay modest coin to mark your progress, without replacing shops, drops, trade, or skilling profit.

Rested command

Run ::rested or ::restedxp to check your current rested pool, its cap, and your earn rate.

Combat and PvP

Sharper fights that never lose Classic timing.

Voidscape keeps the familiar turn rhythm, the inventory pressure, the retreats, the range checks, and the prayer decisions, then builds visual feedback and custom combat spaces around that base.

Overhead protection prayers

Protect from Missiles and the new level 43 Protect from Magic both show overhead bubbles. Ranged uses arrows, magic uses a wizard hat, and only one runs at a time.

Magic and prayer polish

Magic damage, spell pressure, prayer state, and protection feedback all read more clearly through PvM and PvP-style fights.

PK Catching Simulator

Drill Classic catching and movement pressure without putting your normal gear at risk.

Void Arena

Step into controlled deathmatch spaces, challenge other players, and run solo pressure fights from the arena lobby.

  • Combat formula tuned around armour mitigation, with less all-or-nothing burst.
  • Tiered hit splats make your strong hits easy to read.
  • PvP melee momentum rewards sustained pressure on a single target.
  • Projectile kiting improvements cut the awkward extra steps during PvM ranged and spell casts.
  • Void Arena supports ranked-style deathmatch spaces and solo challenge fights.
  • DM King / Sir Charles gives a player-like solo pressure test across food, magic, melee, and prayer decisions.

Skilling and supplies

Skilling with less downtime and the market left intact.

Skilling quality-of-life clears the worst stalls while keeping gathering routes, tool checks, depletion, and the value of player-made supplies.

Guaranteed resource smoothing

Mining, woodcutting, and normal fishing guard against long dry streaks and early node depletion, while keeping the normal action rhythm.

Make-All menus

Cooking and smelting get faster repeated-action menus for long supply sessions.

Player supply loops

Food, arrows, herblaw, firemaking ashes, and ranged supplies are tuned so skillers matter to fighters.
